Project Details

SAVEIT APP

Project Story

SaveIt was created to tackle the lack of accessible financial management tools in Tunisia. Many struggle with managing their finances, leading to issues like debt and an inability to save. Our app offers an intuitive solution that enables users to track expenses, set savings goals, and manage their finances effectively. Participating in the 15th edition of the National Competition "Les Entrepreneurs de Demain" by Injaz Tunisia was a key milestone, allowing us to showcase SaveIt and further our mission of making financial management simple and accessible for everyone.
